Upper body wrkout usually focuses on the upper part of the body and here are my some favourites and most effective workout for upper body .

1. SHOULDER PRESS

Overhead press (abbreviated OHP), also referred to as a shoulder press, military press, or simply the press, is a weight training exercise with many variations. It is typically performed while either standing or sitting sometimes also when squatting, in which a weight is pressed straight upwards from racking position until the arms are locked out overhead, while the legs, lower back and abs maintain balance.[1] The exercise helps build muscular shoulders with bigger arms, and is one of the most difficult compound upper-body exercises
